Dock the Screen, Live the Scene

Being Present is a MagSafe charging dock that encourages healthier phone habits without forcing anything. Docking transforms your phone into a calmer, limited experience (nature, photos, positive prompts) and rewards real presence.

1
Dock your phone
Charging becomes a presence trigger, not another scroll trigger.
2
Earn points for clean minutes
Touch/scroll makes that minute “dirty” and worth 0 points.
3
Time-weighted rewards
Bedtime and school/work windows can give bonus points.
4
Build a daily streak
Hit 60 clean minutes/day (configurable) to keep the streak alive.
Use the Dashboard to simulate docking sessions for your demo.
